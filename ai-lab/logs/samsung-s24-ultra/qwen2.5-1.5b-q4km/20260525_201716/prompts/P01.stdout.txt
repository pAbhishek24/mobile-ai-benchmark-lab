What percentage of my income did I spend in each category? What is my total spend and savings this month? To calculate the percentage of your income spent in each category, we need to divide the amount spent in each category by your total monthly salary and then multiply by 100 to get the percentage.

Here's the calculation:

1. Food: ₹8,200 / ₹55,000 * 100 = 15.18%
2. Transport: ₹3,500 / ₹55,000 * 100 = 6.36%
3. Rent: ₹12,000 / ₹55,000 * 100 = 21.82%

6. Shopping: ₹4,200 / ₹55,000 * 100 = 7.62%

Total spend = ₹8,200 + ₹3,500 + ₹12,000 + ₹2,800 + ₹1,500 + ₹4,200
Total spend = ₹34,200

Total savings = Monthly salary - Total spend
Total savings = ₹55,000 - ₹34,200
