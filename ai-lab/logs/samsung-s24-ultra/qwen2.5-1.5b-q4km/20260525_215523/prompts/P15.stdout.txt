To determine which debt to attack first using the avalanche method, we need to compare the interest rates and the remaining balance of each debt. The avalanche method involves attacking the debt with the highest interest rate first, as it will yield the highest return on the money you're putting towards repayment.

3. **Gold loan: ₹80,000 at 11% annual interest**
   - Remaining balance: ₹80,000
