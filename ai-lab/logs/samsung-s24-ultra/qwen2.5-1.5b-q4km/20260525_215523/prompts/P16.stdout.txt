Should I use the bonus to prepay the home loan, fully close the personal loan, or invest in mutual funds? Justify your recommendation. To determine the best use of the Diwali bonus, we need to compare the cost of borrowing the money versus the potential returns from investing it. We will calculate the interest savings from prepaying the home loan and the personal loan, and then compare it to the potential returns from investing the bonus in mutual funds.

1. **Calculate the total interest on the home loan:**
   \[
   \text{Total interest} = \text{Principal} \times \text{Interest Rate} \times \text{Time}
   \]
   \[
   \text{Total interest} = 32,00,000 \times 0.0875 \times 18 = 5,04,000
   \]

2. **Calculate the interest savings from prepaying the home loan:**
   \[
   \text{Interest savings} = \text{Total interest} - \text{Prepayment amount}

   \[
   \text{Interest savings} = 5,04,000 - 80,000 = 4,24,000
   \]
